# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to making participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards

### Examples of Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment includes:

- **Unwelcome comments** about a person's lifestyle choices, coding practices, or experience level
- **Personal insults** or attacks, especially those using racist or sexist terms
- **Trolling** or intentionally provoking others
- **Sustained disruption** of discussions, threads, or meetings
- **Unwanted sexual attention** or advances
- **Publishing or threatening** private information about others
- **Other unprofessional conduct** that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

### Examples of Acceptable Behavior

- **Respectful disagreement** with others' viewpoints
- **Constructive criticism** focused on code and ideas, not people
- **Helpful and collaborative** attitude
- **Professional communication** in all interactions
- **Acknowledging and learning** from mistakes
- **Focusing on what is best for the community and project

## Responsibilities

### Contributors

- **Be welcoming** to new contributors and community members
- **Be respectful** of different viewpoints and experiences
- **Focus on what is best** for the project and community
- **Accept feedback** gracefully and constructively
- **Assume good intentions** in others' actions
- **Be patient** with newcomers and those learning Rust

### Maintainers

- **Lead by example** in professional conduct
- **Address issues promptly** and professionally
- **Enforce this code of conduct** fairly and consistently
- **Provide clear guidance** when discussing conduct issues
- **Protect privacy** of those reporting issues

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and investigated.

Depending on the severity of the violation, maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

- **Private warning** to clarify that the behavior is inappropriate
- **Request to stop** the problematic behavior
- **Temporary or permanent suspension** of access to project resources
- **Permanent ban** from the project in severe cases

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub issues, discussions, pull requests, etc.)
- All project communication channels (email, chat, etc.)
- All project events (conferences, meetups, etc.)
